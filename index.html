What it does

Session profiles

Save a logged-in session (cookies + local/session storage) and restore it in one click. Jump between admin, user and test accounts on the same site without retyping passwords.

Environment switcher

Move between localhost, staging and production while keeping the same path — /dashboard stays /dashboard across every environment.

Keyboard shortcuts

Restore your first three sessions with Alt+Shift+1/2/3, without even opening the popup.

100% local & private

Your sessions are stored on your device and never sent to any server. No tracking, no ads.
